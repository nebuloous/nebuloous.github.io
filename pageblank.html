Woolypolies are 10-legged, sheep-like beasts that can curl their bodies into a ball. Their natural walking speed is already faster than most sheep, but they can reach far higher speeds by rolling. Unfortunately, that makes them much more dangerous than they seem. They are quite defensive creatures that have no qualms bowling over anything that could be a threat. As such, they are difficult to tame, but that only makes their wool all the more valuable.
